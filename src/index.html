What is Jiu-Jitsu?
A Brief Summary

Jiu-Jitsu is a grappling art founded by the Gracie family back in the early 1900's.
Since then, it has been refined and adapted as a necessary component in the sport of MMA (Mixed Martial Arts)

Applications
  • MMA (Mixed Martial Arts)
  • Law enforcement and Military Training
  • Self-Defense
  • Beating up your friends
Benefits of Practicing Jiu-Jitsu

There are numerous benefits to practicing jiu-jitsu and they include but are not limited to...

  • Great physical exercise for building strength and cardiovascular endurance
  • An excellent atmosphere to socialize and building a sense of community
  • A very useful fighting skill to have, whether it's for self-defense or MMA
